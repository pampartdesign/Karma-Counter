<h1>Karma Counter</h1>

<p>Karma Counter is a playful mobile application that tracks everyday behaviour through humour, gamification and self-reflection.</p>

<p>Users can log good deeds, confess small mistakes and observe how their actions influence their overall moral alignment.</p>

<p>The goal is to create a light but meaningful experience that encourages awareness, kindness and personal growth without pressure.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Concept</h2>

<p>Modern habit tracking focuses mainly on productivity.</p>

<p>Karma Counter focuses on human behaviour.</p>

<p>It highlights:</p>

<ul>
  <li>small acts of kindness</li>
  <li>everyday imperfections</li>
  <li>redemption moments</li>
  <li>behavioural awareness</li>
</ul>

<p>The application presents this through a dark, minimal and slightly quirky interface.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Core Features</h2>

<h3>Good Deeds Tracker</h3>
<p>Users can log positive actions and gradually build their karma score.</p>

<h3>Committed Sins Counter</h3>
<p>Daily mistakes can be recorded to reflect behavioural balance.</p>

<h3>Karma Mood Meter</h3>
<p>A dynamic indicator that reflects the user’s current moral alignment based on recent activity.</p>

<h3>Daily Fate (Karma Lottery)</h3>
<p>A daily spin system that provides challenges, rewards or humorous penalties.</p>

<h3>Badges and Archetypes</h3>
<p>Users unlock titles and achievements based on behavioural patterns.</p>

<h3>Redemption Quests</h3>
<p>Optional daily missions that allow users to improve their karma score.</p>

<h3>Behaviour Analytics</h3>
<p>Simple insights showing trends, streaks and activity patterns.</p>

<h3>Social Confession</h3>
<p>Users can share their karma journey or invite friends to symbolically split mistakes.</p>
