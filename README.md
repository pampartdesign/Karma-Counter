# Karma-Counter
Track your good deeds, confess your daily sins, and discover your spiritual mood with Karma Counter — a quirky and fun mobile app designed to turn self-reflection into a game.  This pre-launch Android version is available for early testers who want to explore the experience and help shape the final release.

Karma Counter – Your Moral Dashboard (Pre-Launch Android Version)
Karma Counter is a playful yet thought-provoking mobile app that helps you track everyday behaviour through humour, gamification and self-awareness.

Instead of productivity metrics, this app focuses on the small human moments — kindness, mistakes, redemption and growth.

This early release APK is provided for testing and feedback before the official launch on app stores.

Key Features
Good Deeds Tracker
Log acts of kindness and build your positive karma over time.

Committed Sins Counter
Confess daily slip-ups and see how they affect your moral balance.

Karma Mood Meter
Understand your current soul status with a dynamic behavioural indicator.

Daily Fate / Karma Lottery
Spin the wheel of destiny and receive quirky daily challenges or rewards.

Badges & Archetypes
Unlock humorous titles and achievements based on your behaviour.

Analytics & Behaviour Insights
View trends, streaks and patterns in your daily actions.

Social Confession & Sin Splitting
Share your karma journey or invite friends to help you redeem your mistakes.
